Product Purpose


We aim to provide a client-facing product that allows end-users to understand how their loan application, or general credit profile would be viewed by prospective institutional lenders.

In order to account for the situational nature of lending practices present in real companies, we will factor in the behavior of fixed income securities markets along with client-provided information.

Factoring in personal information alongside relevant macroeconomic data will allow a client to walk away with a clear understanding of their financial position in the current market climate.
